--- layout: piecrust title: Installation --- 1. Download the [latest version of the sample website][1]. 1. [Change the title][6] of the website. 1. Add/edit some [pages][2] and [layouts][7]. 1. If you'll be using **PieCrust** as a lightweight CMS: 1. Upload the files to your web-server. 1. If you'll be [baking][5] your website: 1. Delete the `index.php` file at the root of the `website` directory. 1. From the `_piecrust` directory, run: `chef bake ../website` 1. Upload the `website/_counter` directory to your web-server. 1. Done!
